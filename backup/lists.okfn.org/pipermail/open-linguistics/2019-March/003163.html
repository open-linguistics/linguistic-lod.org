5 funded PhD positions at the University of Bologna in collaboration with
CNR (STLab) on Humanistic Artificial Intelligence and Semantic Web


We invite expressions of interest for applying to five fully funded 3-year
PhD positions focusing on interdisciplinary research.

The PhD programs will be developed under the context of a collaboration
between University of Bologna and National Research Council (CNR).


Topics belong to one or more of the following:

Knowledge graphs

Ontology patterns

Deep learning applications

Semantic virtual environments

Social intelligence

Cultural Heritage


We welcome candidates with a strong background in at least one of the
following disciplines: Computer Science, Cognitive Sciences, Digital
Humanities, Linguistics.

Please send your expression of interest with a CV to
andreagiovanni.nuzzolese at cnr.it by March 20th, 2019
