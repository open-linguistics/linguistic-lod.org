Dear all,

I just wanted to announce that the program for the LDL-2012 is available  
under http://ldl2012.lod2.eu. LDL will take place from 7th to 9th March  
2012 in the IG-Farben-Haus, Grünburgplatz 1, Frankfurt/M., Germany, room  
IG 0.254.

The registration is handled by the hosting conference, see the pages of  
the DGfS 2012 for details on this, for the venue and for other  
organizational issues: http://dgfs.uni-frankfurt.de/dgfs/dgfs_en.html.

As the LDL is organized by us, it is probably a good idea to have a  
real-life meeting of the Working Group at this occasion. Given the room  
reservation, we have two possibilities:

- March 8th, 04:00 pm, Room IG 0.254 (parallel to the DGfS members'  
meeting)
- March 9th, 02:00 pm, Room IG 0.254 (right after the workshop)

The latter option avoids an overlap with the DGfS schedule and is thus  
preferable. Other opinions ?

Best,
Christian
