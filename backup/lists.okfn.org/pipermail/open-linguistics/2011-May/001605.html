Dear all,
we are happy to inform you that our workshop "Linked Data in Linguistics"  
has been accepted for the 2012 meeting of the DGfS (German equivalent of  
LSA). This means that Linked Data will be present at the most important  
linguistics conference in Germany. This is part of a larger effort of  
establishing Linked Data as a concept in the linguistics world. A call for  
papers with more detailed information will follow in due course

Best wishes

Sebastian Nordhoff
Christian Chiarcos
Sebastian Hellmann


PS; The conference will be held in Frankfurt a.M., Germany on  March 7-9,  
2012
