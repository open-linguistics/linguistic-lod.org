Dear all,

apologies, I just realized I forgot to send out the reminder for today's  
skype call.

Brief summary for those who missed it:

- For the new semester, we will Doodle to find a new time slot as it  
turned out to be inconvenient to some (-> Bettina, later this week)

- We formulated a preliminary consensus on "linguistic relevance" that  
Bettina and me will update until the end of this week in the wiki (a  
modified version of 3a,  
http://wiki.okfn.org/Wg/linguistics/llod-categories).

- Until the next telco, everyone is asked to provide feedback. Unless  
objections are raised, we suggest that the consensus definition will be  
officially adopted by the attendents of the next telco on behalf of the  
OWLG, and published as such over the website.

- With the definition in place, the next goal is to address the primary  
categories for the LLOD diagram. (We also have a consensus that  
different/dynamic color/selection schemes according to additional criteria  
are desirable, but the primary categories are the ones we use to generate  
static and official versions of the diagram.)

Best,
