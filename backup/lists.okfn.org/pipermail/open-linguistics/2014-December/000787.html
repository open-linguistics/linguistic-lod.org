During the last telcos we discussed a new dataset category named 
“linguistically relevant datasets/resources” for datasets such as 
Dbpedia or Ontos News Portal in the cloud. I set up a new wiki page to 
discuss the issue and to collect suggestions for a definition.

Please have a look at the page 
http://wiki.okfn.org/Talk:Wg/linguistics/llod and contribute by making 
an own proposition and/or commenting on existing ones.
