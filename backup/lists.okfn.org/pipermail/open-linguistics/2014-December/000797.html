Dear All,

We would like to inform you that the deadline for submitting papers for the
International Conference on Electrical and Bio-medical Engineering, Clean
Energy and Green Computing (EBECEGC2015) is on 8th January, 2015.

The conference aims to enable researchers build connections between
different digital applications. The event will be held over three days,
with presentations delivered by researchers from the international
community, including presentations from keynote speakers and
state-of-the-art lectures.

For submission of papers, and further details, please visit the conference
website - http://goo.gl/zcvutp

Looking forward to receive your papers related to Electrical/ Bio-medical
Engineering and  Clean Energy/Green Computing.

Best regards,
Hazel Ann
