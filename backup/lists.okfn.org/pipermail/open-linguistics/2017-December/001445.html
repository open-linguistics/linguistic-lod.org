***Jobs: NLP Specialists and Language Engineers at Semalytix***

Semalytix is a growing start-up founded in 2015 as a spin-off of
Bielefeld University. We specialize in developing products for dark data
understanding. Our solutions extract insights in real time from multiple
sources of textual data to support our customers in making better
decisions at the operational and strategic level. Our services apply
state-of-the-art technology from Natural Language Processing, Artificial
Intelligence and Machine Learning.

In order to expand our R&D team, we are seeking to hire NLP specialists
with machine learning expertise and language engineers. Please refer
to the postings below for more details:
