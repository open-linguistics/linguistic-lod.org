We are happy to announce that the OpenMinTeD call for TDM software and
knowledge resources was officially published. We ask ‘service
providers’ (researchers,
SMEs, innovators) to submit a proposal to develop and integrate their
software or knowledge resource into the OpenMinTeD infrastructure. This
means we are more and more in business with the rest of the TDM world. Winners
of this call will be awarded € 7.000 (small bids) or € 17.500 (larger bids)
to implement their plans.


You will find more information on the dedicated blog: https://openminted
.bsc.es/ and on the the OpenMinTeDwebsite: http://openminted.eu/join-call-td
m-software-knowledge-resources/


The deadline for proposals is 14 January 2018.


Will you please spread the word among everyone in your network who could be
interested?
