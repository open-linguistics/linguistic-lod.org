Changelog (09/05/12):    -IDS Dataset was converted, awaiting validation 
and interlinking

awaiting validation and hosting
                                         -Gold and LexInfo validated,

                   (08/22/12):    -Links which were not validated are 
now dashed lines

To do:

  * Hosting becomes an issue as some datasets have valid data but are
    not hosted
  * I was unable to find a reason why OLiA Annotation model is not valid.
  * I think we should put some thought into wiktionary: A lot of
    use-cases might depend on a multilingual dictionary. Before it went
    down, many word forms were not available as linked data at
    wiktionary.dbpedia.org. Additional language mappings would also help
    very much.
  * A general discussion about use-cases would be very helpful to
    prepare for the hackday.
