Dear Open Linguists,

as many of us are specifically interested in areas at the intersection of
information technology and linguistics, I'd like to make you aware of the
following job offer from the University of Gothenburg. Please disseminate !

Best,
Christian

The Department of Swedish at the University of Gothenburg has a vacancy for
one or more postdoctoral fellow positions (one year, with a possible
extension of up to one additional year) in Language Technology. The
successful candidates will be placed in the Språkbanken language technology
group <http://spraakbanken.gu.se> and participate in the activities of the
Centre for Language Technology <http://www.clt.gu.se>, a high-profile
cross-disciplinary environment for research collaboration in language
technology, organizers of the successful EACL 2014 conference.

The successful applicant will conduct their research in the project "South
Asia as a linguistic area? Exploring big-data methods in areal and genetic
linguistics" <http://spraakbanken.gu.se/eng/research/lsi>, and specifically
work on developing automatic information extraction and text mining methods
for turning the content of conventional descriptive grammars (in free text)
into formally structured information in a typological database. The source
material will be in English, primarily the 268 grammar sketches from the
Linguistic Survey of India will be targeted.

Closing date: 10th April, 2015.

For more information and online application forms, see
