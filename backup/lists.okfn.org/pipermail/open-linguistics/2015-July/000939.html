Engineering, Computer Networks, and Social Media Conference

September 25-27, 2015
Technological Educational Institute of Western Macedonia,Kastoria, Greece

http://sdiwc.net/conferences/seeda-cecnsm2015/

IMPORTANT DATES:

Submission Deadline          : Open from now until August 25, 2015
Notification of Acceptance   : 3- 4 weeks from the submission date
Camera Ready Submission  : Open from now until September 15, 2015
Registration Deadline           : Open from now until September 15, 2015
