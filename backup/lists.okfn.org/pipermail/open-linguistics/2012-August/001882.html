We are pleased to announce that the Linguistic Annotation Framework (LAF)
and its XML serialization in the Graph Annotation Format (GrAF) are now
published as an ISO standard (ISO 24612). To obtain a copy, please see
http://www.iso.org/iso/catalogue_detail.htm?csnumber=37326 or contact
ide at cs.vassar.edu.
