** You are invited to participate in The Fourth International Conference on Informatics & Applications (ICIA2015) that will be held in Takamatsu, Japan, on July 20-22, 2015. The event will be held over three days, with presentations delivered by researchers from the international community, including presentations from keynote speakers and state-of-the-art lectures.
------------------------------------------------------------
