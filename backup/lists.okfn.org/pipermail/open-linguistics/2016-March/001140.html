Call for contributions/reviewers/papers



*** Please accept our apologies if you receive multiple copies of this CFP
***



IEEE SEEDA-CECNSM 2016



Please submit your recent work and/or forward this CFP to any of your
colleagues, friends that might be interested.



The South-East Europe Design Automation, Computer Engineering, Computer
Networks, and Social Media Conference

(SEEDA-CECNSM 2016)



Venue:

Technological Educational Institute of Western Macedonia

Kastoria, Greece

September 25-27, 2016



web-page:

http://kastoria.teiwm.gr/seeda



================================================================================



The event will be held over three days, with presentations delivered by
researchers and excellent scholars from the



international community, including presentations from keynote speakers and
state-of-the-art lectures. The

conference



aims to enable researchers build connections between different digital
application/methodology areas.





TOPIC AREA

==========

- Design Automation

- Computer Networks

- Computer Engineering

- Social Media





SUBMISSION

==========

- Submitted paper should be from 4 to 8 pages.

- The submission must be for the full paper (abstracts only are not
acceptable).

- All papers will be thoroughly reviewed by experts in their domain

- Researchers are encouraged to submit their work electronically through:



       http://kastoria.teiwm.gr/seeda/paper-submission/

- All accepted papers will be published by IEEE CPS (IEEExplore included)





PUBLICATION

===========



- All registered papers will be published electronically in SEEDA
proceedings





IMPORTANT DATES

===============



Submission Deadline :                                                   1
May 2016

Notification of acceptance/rejection :                    30 May 2016 or 2
weeks after submission (the earliest)

camera ready submission                                            10 June
2016

(early) Registration :
20 June 2016

Conference :
25-27 September 2016





Please answer to me with a notification email if you intend to submit to
this excellent conference.



Best regards,



Dr Michael Dossis

Associate Professor

General Chair

SEEDA-CECNSM 2016
