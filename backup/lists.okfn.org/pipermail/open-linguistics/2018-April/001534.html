Holistic Benchmarking of Big Linked Data

Dear all,

We invite you to respond to the joint survey of HOBBIT (https://project-hobbit.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=00d040a1bedee64c86d8911b0&id=b4ecd22ffa&e=ba2124fabf) and BDVA (https://project-hobbit.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=00d040a1bedee64c86d8911b0&id=623a201279&e=ba2124fabf) on generic benchmarking needs in the context of BDVA Benchmarking SG.

By answering this questionnaire, you will help gathering evidence on the use of Big Data technologies and benchmarks. With this survey, we aim to assess how companies could benefit from Big Data benchmarking. The results will be used to build a bridge between technical and business benchmarking. All results will be shared with registered respondents.
Please fill out the survey: https://project-hobbit.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=00d040a1bedee64c86d8911b0&id=722282ee87&e=ba2124fabf

On behalf of the HOBBIT team
