The Fourth International Conference on Digital Information and
Communication Technology and its Applications (DICTAP 2014)



University of the Thai Chamber of Commerce, Bangkok, Thailand

May 6-8, 2014



http://sdiwc.net/conferences/2014/dictap2014/



The conference is technically co-sponsored by IEEE Thailand. All
registered papers will be submitted to IEEE for inclusion to IEEE
Xplore.



Emerging information technologies offer new answers to the humanity,
improving methods, enabling automation and productive decision-making
mechanisms and altering current ways of work. Research has tended to
address a direct causal relationship between the emerging and potential
information technologies and the future of humanity.



The research in data expertise initiatives will substantially aid in
enhancing the quality of information expertise support supplied to the
distinct localities and domains. Therefore this conference will present a
scenario-planning workout that directed to identify possible future
information technologies that the humanity would face.



All submitted papers will be reviewed by a minimum of two reviewers.



BEST registered papers will be published in one of the following special
issues provided that the author do major improvements and extension within
the time frame that will be set by the conference and his/her paper is
approved by the chief editor:



+ IJNCAA (ISSN: 2220-9085; DOAJ, Cabell's Directories)

+ IJDIWC (ISSN: 2225-658X; IndexCopernicus, DOAJ, etc.)

+ IJCSDF (ISSN: 2305-0012; Scirus, EBSCOhost, etc.)

+ IJEEI (ISSN: 1947-8585; Cabell's Directories, DBLP, etc.)

+ IJESMA (ISSN: 1941-627X; Ulrich's Periodicals Directory, etc.)





Paper Submission Deadline: April 20, 2014

Notification of Acceptance: April 23, 2014

Camera Ready Submission: April 28, 2014

Registration Deadline: April 28, 2014

Conference Dates: May 6-8, 2014
