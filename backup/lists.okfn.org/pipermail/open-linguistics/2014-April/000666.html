Dear all,

at the last telco, it was suggested to shift to a fixed schedule for  
telcos (to be applied as of June).

As for the time, it was suggested to keep the 02:00pm slot (CET/Berlin  
time) we had for the last meetings.
Would that work for everyone? If not, we may also alternate between two or  
three time slots to accomodate people from either the Americas on the one  
hand or Asia and the Pacific on the other hand or use these upon  
demand/request.

I prepared a Doodle with two parameters
- rhythm (2 weeks, 4 weeks, 6 weeks), and
- day (Mo, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fr)

Please vote under http://doodle.com/hpvuufwfd2ry9tdf, and please don't  
hesitate with any feedback.

We will make a decision at the next telco. With day and rhythm fixed, I  
would suggest to start in the first week of June.
