Dear all,
the workshop "Linked Data in Linguistic Typology" will take place on the
afternoon of August 15, under the umbrella of the 10th meeting of the
Association for Linguistic Typology.

http://www.eva.mpg.de/lingua/conference/2013_ALT10/files/theme_sessions.html#session4


Preliminary program:

15.25-15.55
Steven Moran (N1)
Typology with graphs and matrices

16.00-16.30	
Jeff Good (N2)
Fine-grained typological investigation of grammatical constructions using
Linked Data

17.00-17.30
Sebastian Nordhoff (N3)
Crowdsourcing WALS

17.35-18.05	
Dorothee Beermann & Pavel Mihaylov (N4)
Linguistic Annotations and Knowledge Representation

18.10-18.40
Mariya Shmatova (N5)
Typological NNC-Database: Storage of Cross-Linguistic Data

Registration is only available or the whole conference at 50 EUR (reduced
40 EUR)
