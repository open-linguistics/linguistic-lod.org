[DINWC 2016] The Fourth International Conference on Digital Information, 
Networking, and Wireless Communications

All registered papers will be submitted to IEEE for potential inclusion
to IEEE Xplore

Conference Dates : July 26-28, 2016
Submission Deadline : June 26, 2016
Notification of Acceptance : July 5, 2016 or 4 weeks from the submission date
Camera Ready Submission and Registration : Open until July 16, 2016
****************************************************************************

For more information: http://sdiwc.net/conferences/dinwc2016/

To submit your paper: http://sdiwc.net/conferences/dinwc2016/openconf/openconf.php
****************************************************************************

This conference, among other things, wants to bring together researchers to present and discuss novel approaches and solutions as well as recent results in this area of research, e.g. Cloud Computing,
Coding for Wireless Systems, Computational Intelligence, Cryptography and Data Protection, Data Mining, E-Government, E-Learning, E-Technology, Embedded Systems and Software, Green Computing, Information Technology Strategies, Mobile Social Networks, Mobile, Ad Hoc and Sensor Network Management, Multimedia Computing, Signal Processing Techniques and Tools, Technology Developments, Wireless Communications and Ad-hoc Networks
****************************************************************************

Submitted papers will be reviewed by the conference Program Committee and judged on originality, technical correctness, relevance, and quality of presentation. An accepted paper must be presented at the CyberSec2016 venue by one of the authors registered at the full registration rate. All registered papers will be published in one of the following special issues provided that the author do major improvements and extension within the time frame that will be set by the conference and his/her paper is approved by the chief editor:

International Journal of New Computer Architectures and their Applications (IJNCAA)
International Journal of Digital Information and Wireless Communications (IJDIWC)
International Journal of Cyber-Security and Digital Forensics (IJCSDF)
International Journal of E-Learning and Educational Technologies in the Digital Media (IJEETDM)
****************************************************************************

*Publication Chairs:
