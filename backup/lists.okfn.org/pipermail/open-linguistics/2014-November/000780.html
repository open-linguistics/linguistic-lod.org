The Second International Conference on Information Security and Digital
Forensics (ISDF2015)

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

All registered papers will be included in SDIWC Digital Library.

Important Dates:

Submission Dates         : September 27, 2015
Notification of Acceptance : 3- 4 weeks from the submission date
Camera Ready Submission : Open from now until October 17, 2015
Registration : Open from now until October 17, 2015

will be fully refereed by a minimum of two specialized referees.
