Dear Madam/ Sir

We are delighted to invite you to participate in *2014 International
Conference on Innovative Applications of Computational Intelligence on
Power, Energy and Controls with their impact on Humanity (CIPECH-14) *   at
Krishna institute of Engineering and Technology Ghaziabad, India on November
28-29, 2014.

*The conference will feature original research papers on*:



   - Computational Intelligence and Applications
   - Control Systems and Applications
   - Power Electronics Drives and Electromechanical Energy Conversion
   - Power System Technology
   - Applied Technologies in Renewable Energy and Smart Grids

*The Conference has Special Session for following:*

   - Emerging Trends in On-Chip Communications
   - Technological Developments in Energy Management
   - Recent Technological Developments in VLSI Design and Tool Automation

 *Paper Submission:* CIPECH-14 is using Easy Chair for submission and paper
processing.
