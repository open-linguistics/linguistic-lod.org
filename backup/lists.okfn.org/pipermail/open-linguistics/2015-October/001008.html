Hello all,

I am organizing a Seminar (of the Society for Language Resources and
Technology of the University of Belgrade, Serbia) about LLOD and basically
different topics that were covered at EUROLAN. It will be held on October
22nd at the Faculty of Mathematics.
Thank you all again for all the wonderful lectures and tutorials that you
gave in Sibiu. Hopefully, we will include the Serbian WordNet and the
Ontology of Rhetorical Figures into the LLOD cloud soon and maybe work on
something interesting together.

Best Regards,
Jelena
