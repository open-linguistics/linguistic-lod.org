Dear members of the list,

The 12th International Conference on Terminology and Knowledge Engineering
(TKE 2016) will take place 22nd – 24th June 2016 at the Copenhagen Business
School (Denmark).

The theme of the conference this year will be: *Term Bases and Linguistic
Linked Open Data*.

The CALL FOR PAPERS AND WORKSHOP PROPOSALS is now posted at the conference
web site:
http://sf.cbs.dk/gtw/conferences_terminology_and_knowledge_engineering/tke_2016_copenhagen
.

We look forward to receiving your contributions.

IMPORTANT DATES:
10th December 2015                Submission of workshop proposals
11th January 2016                   Notification of acceptance of workshop
proposals
1st March 2016                       Submission of papers
16th April 2016                        Notification of acceptance of papers
20th May 2016                        Final versions for the proceedings
22nd-24th June 2016               Conference & workshops

Please, feel free to forward this message ;-).

Best regards,

The organizers:
Hanne Erdman Thomsen, Copenhagen Business School
Bodil Nistrup Madsen, Copenhagen Business School
Antonio Pareja-Lora, Universidad Complutense de Madrid
