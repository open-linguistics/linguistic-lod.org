Hello all,

I would just quickly like to introduce myself to the list. I have been a
post-doc at CITEC, University of Bielefeld, Germany since 2009, working with
Philipp Cimiano and Dennis Spohr

I am part of the Monnet <http://www.monnet-project.eu/> (Multilingual
Ontologies) project and as such am very interested in the representation of
multilingual information on the semantic web. Currently, we have been
working on the lemon + LexInfo models which aim to provide powerful
representations of lexical information on the semantic web, more details are
available at http://www.lexinfo.net/

Hopefully, we can discuss some of the interesting challenges here of
providing open language resources.

I would also like to thank Sebastian Hellmann for the introduction
