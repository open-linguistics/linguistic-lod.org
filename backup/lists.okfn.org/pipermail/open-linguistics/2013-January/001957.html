Dear colleagues,

I am writing from the Institute of the Lithuanian Language and as one of representatives of META-NORD (Baltic and Nordic Parts of the European Open Linguistic Infrastructure) project in Lithuania. As it is obvious from the full name of the project, it is dedicated  for Baltic and Scandinavian countries having less than 10 mln. speakers.

The aim of the project is to create open linguistic infrastructure for share and develop language recourses and tools and serve the needs of European industry and research communities. More about the project you can read: http://meta-nord.eu/ and in the newsletters: http://meta-nord.eu/index.php?p=newsletter-2.

Im writing in order to find out if your community could be interested in the project. Maybe there is possibility to collaborate or to share the information about META-NORD for open data community?

I'm looking forward to hearing from you,

With best regards,
