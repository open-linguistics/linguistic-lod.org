The Fourth International Conference on Digital Information Processing, 
Data Mining and Wireless Communication (DIPDMWC2017)

URL: 
http://sdiwc.net/conferences/4th-international-conference-on-digital-information-processing-data-mining-and-wireless-communication/
DATES: July 19-20, 2017
VENUE: Universitas Mercu Buana - Meruya Campus, Jakarta, Indonesia


Publication
===========
All papers accepted, registered and presented will be submitted to IEEE 
for potential inclusion to IEEE Xplore.


Topics
======
Topics are not limited to Digital Information Processing, Data Mining, 
Wireless Communications, Expert Systems, Image Processing, E-Commerce, 
Computer-Aided Surgery, Data Mining Techniques, Text and Web Mining, 
Coding and Modulation, Mobile Database Access and Design and many more..


Paper Submission
================
Researchers are encouraged to submit their work electronically. 
Submitted paper should not exceed 15 pages, including illustrations and 
it must be without page numbers.


Full Paper Submission Deadline
=============================
May 19, 2017
