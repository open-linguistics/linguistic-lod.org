Dear all,

while starting to prepare the new LLOD cloud diagram, I thought it might  
be a good idea to integrate relevant data from the Share your Resources  
initiative of the upcoming LREC conference  
(http://lrec2014.lrec-conf.org/en/calls-for-papers/lrec-2014-special-highlight/).  
I just began creating datahub.io entries for the LDL-2014 submissions, but  
the full set of LREC resources is far beyond my capabilities at the  
moment. However, if a few people volunteer to invest half an hour of their  
time and add one data set or another to Datahub.io (it takes up to 10  
minutes per data set), it's a feasible task to get reasonable coverage.  
So, please let me know if you're interested.

Actually, this may also be a chance to increase the visibility of the OWLG  
once more: With a plain description of experiences and problems  
encountered in the process we can participate in the LREC Open Challenge  
on Shared Language Resources  
(http://www.resourcebook.eu/shareyourlr/challenge.pdf, due May 2nd),  
present our work with a poster and contribute to the challenge proceedings.

Best,
Christian
