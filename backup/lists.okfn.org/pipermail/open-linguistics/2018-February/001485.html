Holistic Benchmarking of Big Linked Data

Dear all,

The training phase for StreamML Open Challenge (https://project-hobbit.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=00d040a1bedee64c86d8911b0&id=79f1efdfc9&e=ba2124fabf) was started. Now participants are able to test their systems online (https://project-hobbit.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=00d040a1bedee64c86d8911b0&id=bca5b1e76a&e=ba2124fabf) under workload of the StreaML Open Challenge Benchmark (StreaML v1.1) and register them (https://project-hobbit.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=00d040a1bedee64c86d8911b0&id=6e0232529f&e=ba2124fabf) for the first task of the StreaML Open Challenge. Please find the example of HOBBIT-compatible samples and other helpful instructions here (https://project-hobbit.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=00d040a1bedee64c86d8911b0&id=d695efb45d&e=ba2124fabf) .

For detailed information, including datasets and submission guidelines,
please visit the challenge website:
https://project-hobbit.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=00d040a1bedee64c86d8911b0&id=363fd95ef6&e=ba2124fabf
Feel free to join StreaML open challenge google group to ask any questions: https://project-hobbit.us12.list-manage.com/track/click?u=00d040a1bedee64c86d8911b0&id=19fe065d0a&e=ba2124fabf
