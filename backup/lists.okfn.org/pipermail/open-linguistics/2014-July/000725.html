Dear Madam/ Sir

We are delighted to invite you to participate in 2014 International
Conference on Innovative Applications of Computational Intelligence on
Power, Energy and Controls with their impact on Humanity
(CIPECH-14) at Krishna institute of Engineering and Technology
Ghaziabad, India on November 28-29, 2014.

The conference will feature original research papers on:

Ø Computational Intelligence and Applications

Ø Control Systems and Applications

Ø Power Electronics Drives and Electromechanical Energy Conversion

Ø Power System Technology

Ø Applied Technologies in Renewable Energy and Smart Grids

The Conference has Special Session for following:

Ø Emerging Trends in On-Chip Communications

Ø Technological Developments in Energy Management

Ø Recent Technological Developments in VLSI Design and Tool
Automation

Paper Submission: CIPECH-14 is using Easy Chair for submission and
paper processing.
